## Overview

Александр Васильевич provides a centralized space for you to create, organize, and manage all your project documentation. You can build structured docs sites with MDX support, version history, and seamless collaboration features. Focus on content while the platform handles hosting, search, and navigation automatically.

<Callout kind="info">
  This documentation space serves as your default hub. Customize it to fit your project's needs.
</Callout>

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Page" icon="file-plus">
    Create `introduction.mdx` with the frontmatter structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Components" icon="settings">

  </Step>
