title: How do I add a new project?
description: Quick steps to add a project in Александр Васильевич.
---

## Steps

1. Log in to your Александр Васильевич dashboard.
2. Click the "+ New Project" button in the sidebar.
3. Enter a project name and description.
4. Optionally upload a logo.
5. Click "Create" to finish.

## Tips

- You can edit project details later.
- All your projects are listed on the dashboard homepage.
