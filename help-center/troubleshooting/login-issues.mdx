## Issue
- Login page reloads without error
- No access to your dashboard

## Possible Causes
- Incorrect email or password
- Browser cache issue

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Double-check your email and password. Use the "Forgot password?" link if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cookies/cache or try a different browser.
  </Step>
</Steps>
